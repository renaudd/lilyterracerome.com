Getting Around

The Art of Walking

From Lily Terrace, the most significant sites of Rome are within an effortless 15-minute walk. Piazza Navona, the Pantheon, and Trastevere are all easily reachable by foot. We suggest comfortable but elegant footwear to navigate the historic Sampietrini cobble stones.

Express to Termini

For a direct connection to Roma Termini station, we recommend Bus 40 (Express) from Corso Vittorio Emanuele II. It avoids the local stops of the 64 and provides a swift 20-minute transit to the main rail hub.

The "Zero Stress" Arrival

From Fiumicino (FCO), strictly follow signs to the official white taxi stand. Ignore anyone inside the terminal offering a ride. The fare is fixed at €55 including luggage. Confirm this with the driver by asking "Tariffa fissa?" before departing. For a "Smart" alternative, take the FL1 Regional Train to Stazione Trastevere (€8) then Tram 8 to Arenula/Cairoli, which avoids the chaos of Termini.

Uber & Local Taxis

Uber Black is highly active and reliable in central Rome. For a traditional taxi, the stand at Largo di Torre Argentina is just a 5-minute walk away.

A Naming Mystery

Why is Rione Regola called "Regola"?

The name comes from Arenula (little sand). Before the Tiber's high walls were built, the river would deposit fine silt here during floods, defining the ground you're walking on today.
