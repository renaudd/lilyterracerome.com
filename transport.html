Getting Around

The Art of Walking

From Lily Terrace, the most significant sites of Rome are within an effortless 15-minute walk. Piazza Navona, the Pantheon, and Trastevere are all easily reachable by foot. We suggest comfortable but elegant footwear to navigate the historic Sampietrini cobble stones.

Private Chauffeur (NCC)

For trips outside the city center or for seamless airport transfers, we recommend Noleggio Con Conducente (NCC). This premium service ensures privacy, comfort, and direct access to areas often restricted to public taxis.

Crossing the Tiber

A short walk across the Ponte Sisto connects you directly to Trastevere. This historic bridge provides one of the most beautiful views of St. Peter's Dome at dusk.
