Historic Elegance in the Heart of Regola.

Occupying the upper levels of a historic palazzo, Lily Terrace epitomizes the timeless sophistication of Rome. Formerly the residence of legendary Spanish painter Ramón Gaya, the residence offers a masterful blend of heritage and contemporary luxury.

A stone's throw from Campo de' Fiori, the property is defined by extraordinary light, sweeping vistas across Roman rooftops, and an atmosphere of profound serenity.
