Planning Your Stay

Seasonal Nuance

To experience Lily Terrace at its peak, we suggest visiting in late April through June, or late September through October. During these months, the Roman light is at its most golden, and the terrace breeze is most cooling.

Regola Etiquette

While Rome is welcoming, our rione values understated elegance. When visiting the nearby Basilica of San Lorenzo in Damaso, remember to dress respectfully. A light linen shawl or jacket is perfect for transitioning from the Roman heat to the quiet cool of historic stone.

Arrival Logistics

We highly recommend arranging a private NCC car for your arrival from Fiumicino. The lanes of Regola are historic and narrow; a professional driver knows exactly how to navigate the final steps to Vicolo del Giglio with grace.
